# Calbright College Brand Guidelines Skill

Apply Calbright College's visual identity and brand voice consistently across all deliverables.

## When to Use This Skill

Use whenever:
- Creating presentations, documents, reports, spreadsheets, or web content for Calbright College
- Writing or editing any text that represents Calbright (marketing copy, emails, social posts, press materials)
- Formatting existing materials to match the Calbright brand
- User mentions Calbright brand, colors, fonts, logo, style, or tone
- Building HTML artifacts, landing pages, or digital assets for Calbright

---

## Part 1: Brand Identity & Mission

### About Calbright College

Calbright College is California's first fully online community college, committed to increasing economic mobility and closing equity gaps for working adults who lack easy access to traditional forms of higher education. The College offers online, flexible, affordable, skills-based programs that provide tangible economic value for both working adults and hiring managers.

### Key Messaging Points

- **Free for adult Californians**: Tuition and fees are currently free
- **Flexible and online**: Programs are online, self-paced, and accessible anywhere, anytime
- **Career-focused**: Programs develop skills for in-demand jobs in IT, cybersecurity, data analytics, project management, CRM administration, and more
- **Part of California Community Colleges**: A proud member of the California Community Colleges system
- **Student-centered**: Personalized support from career coaching to academic counseling

### Brand Essence

**"Welcoming Spirit"** — The Calbright brand conveys warmth, friendliness, optimism, openness, and positivity. All materials should feel inviting and accessible to working adults from all backgrounds across California.

### Brand Voice & Tone

- **Warm and welcoming**: Speak to students as people, not applicants
- **Optimistic and empowering**: Focus on possibilities and potential
- **Clear and accessible**: Use plain language; avoid academic jargon
- **Inclusive**: Reflect the diversity of California's working adults
- **Encouraging**: Support the student's journey without being patronizing

### Official Naming

- **Full name**: "Calbright College" (use on first reference and formal contexts)
- **Short name**: "Calbright" (acceptable on second and subsequent references)
- **Social media handle**: @gocalbright (Instagram, Threads, Facebook), @calbright-college (LinkedIn)
- **Media contact**: pr@calbright.org

## Part 2: Visual Brand Identity

### Color Palette

The template uses a focused palette. The slide reference (slide 28 of the official deck) defines these as the "main color values":

**Primary Colors** (main brand elements):
- Sky Blue: #1A23B3 — Primary blue for backgrounds, headers, accent shapes, and CTAs
- Sunset Orange: #D34508 — The brand's warm accent for highlights, subtitles, callouts, strikethrough comparisons, doodles, and the "College" text in the logo
- Sunrise Yellow / Gold: #FFC629 — Warm gold accent for the California symbol, highlights, and emphasis

**Neutral & Background Colors**:
- Midnight Black (Dark Grey): #1D1B1D — Headlines, primary text, dark backgrounds
- Moon Gray (Med Grey): #504F55 — Body copy, secondary text
- Light Gray: #E0E0E0 — Borders, dividers, subtle UI elements
- Beige / Cream 3: #B2A490 — Warm neutral for callout backgrounds and accent bands
- Med Cream / Cream 2: #F5EEE4 (also seen as #F4EEE5 in slides) — Warm background for callout sections and 50/50 splits
- Lt Cream / Cream 1: #FFFCF8 — Near-white warm page/slide background
- White: #FFFFFF — Clean backgrounds, text on dark surfaces

**Image Overlays** (applied over photography):
- Overlay 1: #1A1A1D at 30% opacity — dark warm overlay
- Overlay 2: #1A23B3 at 15% opacity — blue-tinted overlay

**Accent colors from the theme** (use sparingly for charts/data viz):
- Teal: #0097A7 — Used for hyperlinks and secondary accents
- Grey accent: #78909C — Muted accent for supporting elements

### Typography

The template's principal font is **Libre Franklin** in multiple weights (the deck itself states this on slide 27). Body text uses Libre Franklin or Roboto/Helvetica Neue (available as embedded fallbacks in the template).

**Heading font**: Libre Franklin (Google Fonts)
- H1: SemiBold 600, large — for slide titles and hero text
- H2: SemiBold 600, medium — for section headers
- H3: Medium 500 — for sub-sections
- H4: Medium 500 — for card headers and smaller callouts
- H5: Bold 700 — for labels and small emphasis
- H6: SemiBold 600 — for captions and metadata

**Body font**: Inter (Google Fonts) or Roboto as fallback
- Regular 400 for all paragraph and body text
- The template also embeds Helvetica Neue and Roboto as system fallbacks

**Presentation-specific sizes** (from slide 27 reference):
- Slide titles: Libre Franklin SemiBold, ~28-36pt equivalent
- Subtitle/date: Libre Franklin SemiBold, Sunset Orange (#D34508), smaller
- Body text: Libre Franklin Regular or Medium, ~14-18pt equivalent
- Callout stats/numbers: Large bold for impact

**Fallback stack**: `'Libre Franklin', 'Roboto', 'Helvetica Neue', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif`

### Logo

The primary logo is a lockup: the "CALBRIGHT" logotype with the California State Star symbol (golden California silhouette topped by an orange star), and "COLLEGE" below in Sunset Orange.

**Logo files bundled in `assets/`:**

| File | Description | Use on |
|------|-------------|--------|
| `calbright-logo-primary.png` | Full logo — black "CALBRIGHT", orange+gold symbol, orange "COLLEGE" | White or light backgrounds (Cream 1, White) |
| `calbright-logo-white.png` | Full logo in white | Dark backgrounds (Sky Blue, Midnight Black) |
| `calbright-logo-white-symbol.png` | White symbol + "COLLEGE" only (no "CALBRIGHT" text) | Dark backgrounds where logotype is separate |

**Clear Space**: The height of the "C" in "CALBRIGHT" defines the minimum clear space on all sides.

**Minimum Size**: 0.60" height (print), 40px height (digital).

### Symbol (State Star)

The standalone symbol — a golden California silhouette with a star — is used when space doesn't allow the full logo (favicons, watermarks, social media profile images). Do not use the symbol and the full logo in the same layout.

### Logo Don'ts

- Do not place the logo over a pattern or busy background without an opacity filter
- Do not distort, skew, or alter proportions
- Do not change the proportions of the symbol relative to the text
- Do not add a drop shadow or stroke
- Do not alter the approved logo colors
- Do not place the logo on a same-colored background (poor contrast)
- Internally, use the white logo on Sky Blue or Sunset Orange
- Externally, use the white logo on contrasting colors

### Hand-Drawn Doodle Illustrations

A key Calbright brand element is a library of **hand-drawn doodle illustrations** in Sunset Orange (#D34508). These informal, sketchy line drawings reinforce the "Welcoming Spirit" — they make materials feel friendly, human, and approachable rather than corporate or institutional.

**Bundled doodle files in `assets/doodles/`:**

| File | Subject |
|------|---------|
| `doodle-pen-swoosh.png` | Pen with flowing line — great for writing/learning themes |
| `doodle-spiral-arrow.png` | Spiral arrow pointing down — directional emphasis |
| `doodle-hearts.png` | Two hearts — community, support, care |
| `doodle-confetti.png` | Falling confetti/ovals — celebration, achievement |
| `doodle-speech-bubbles.png` | Question + exclamation speech bubbles — dialogue, Q&A |
| `doodle-stars.png` | Three stars of different sizes — achievement, aspiration |
| `doodle-swirl-arrow.png` | Dashed swirl with downward loop — flow, process |

**How to use doodles:**
- Place doodles in corners, margins, or near callouts as decorative accents
- They work best at moderate sizes, not filling entire slide areas
- Use 1-2 doodles per slide/page maximum — they lose impact if overused
- All doodles are in Sunset Orange on transparent backgrounds so they layer easily on white, cream, or light backgrounds
- On dark backgrounds (Sky Blue, Midnight Black), the orange doodles still provide strong contrast

### Hand-Drawn Icon Sets

The brand includes a full library of **hand-drawn icons** in two colorways — Sunset Orange and Sky Blue. These match the doodle illustration style (thick, friendly, sketch-like linework) and are used for content slides, feature callouts, and infographic elements.

**Bundled samples in `assets/icons-orange/` and `assets/icons-blue/`:**
- Each folder contains a representative sample of 6 icons
- The full set in the template includes ~38 icons per colorway covering topics like: documents, laptops, lightbulbs, charts, people, communication, tools, awards, and more
- Every orange icon has a matching blue icon — pick the colorway based on your background

**When to use which colorway:**
- **Orange icons** (#D34508): On white, cream, or light gray backgrounds
- **Blue icons** (#1A23B3): On white, cream, or light gray backgrounds when you want to vary from orange, or when orange is already prominent

### Branded Quote Marks

A large decorative **blue quotation mark** (`assets/calbright-quotemark-blue.png`) in Sky Blue (#1A23B3) is available for quote/testimonial callout slides. Place it behind or beside quoted text as a large background element.

### Photography Style

Photos should reflect the "Welcoming Spirit":
- **Mood**: Warm, friendly, fun, open, optimistic, and positive
- **Subject matter**: Diverse working adults learning, collaborating, and succeeding
- **Setting**: Professional/educational environments, tech in use, people with laptops
- **Diversity**: Reflect California's broad population
- **Treatment**: Apply brand overlays (#1A1A1D at 30% or #1A23B3 at 15%) for cohesion

## Part 3: Presentation Template — Slide Types & Layout Patterns

The official Calbright presentation template is a **widescreen 10" x 5.63"** (16:9) deck. Understanding the slide types helps reproduce them consistently. The template is organized on Google Slides and has been exported to PPTX.

### Slide Type 1: Title Slide

- **Background**: White/cream, or full-bleed photo with overlay
- **Title**: Libre Franklin SemiBold, large, Midnight Black (#1D1B1D)
- **Subtitle**: Libre Franklin SemiBold, Sunset Orange (#D34508), smaller
- **Date/meta**: Libre Franklin Regular, medium gray (#898989)
- **Logo**: Primary logo in lower-left or corner area
- **Doodles**: Optional — a single doodle in the corner adds warmth

### Slide Type 2: Full-Width Text / Content Slide

- **Background**: White or Lt Cream (#FFFCF8)
- **Section title**: Libre Franklin SemiBold, Midnight Black, top area
- **Body text**: Libre Franklin Regular/Medium, Moon Gray (#504F55)
- **Accent bands**: Beige (#B2A490) or Light Gray (#E0E0E0) horizontal bands behind content groups
- **Used for**: Stats, funnel diagrams, multi-point layouts, data tables

### Slide Type 3: 50/50 Split — Image + Text

- **Layout**: Left half = photo (with brand overlay), Right half = text on cream/white, or vice versa
- **Photo side**: Full-bleed image with Overlay 1 (#1A1A1D at 30%) or Overlay 2 (#1A23B3 at 15%)
- **Text side**: Background in Med Cream (#F5EEE4) or White
- **Title on text side**: Libre Franklin SemiBold, Midnight Black
- **Logo or doodle**: Small doodle or Calbright icon in photo corner

### Slide Type 4: 50/50 Split — Callout + Text

- **Layout**: Left half = solid Sky Blue (#1A23B3) or Med Cream (#F5EEE4), Right half = contrasting color
- **Callout side**: Large stat, quote, or key phrase in white (on blue) or Midnight Black (on cream)
- **Text side**: Supporting detail in body text
- **Use**: Highlight a key number, quote, or takeaway

### Slide Type 5: Three Boxes Across

- **Layout**: Three equal columns with images or colored boxes
- **Image variant**: Three photos cropped into rounded-corner rectangles with text overlaid
- **Color variant**: Three boxes in Sunset Orange (#D34508) outline on gray (#ADADAE) background, with Sky Blue (#1A23B3) accents for numbers/labels
- **Use**: Three-point comparisons, features, or program highlights

### Slide Type 6: Image Transition / Section Divider

- **Layout**: Full-bleed photograph covering entire slide
- **Overlay**: Apply brand overlay for readability
- **Text**: Simple section title in white, centered, Libre Franklin SemiBold
- **The template includes 13 image transition variants** using different photographs — these break up content sections and add visual rhythm
- **Logo**: Sometimes a small white logo is placed in the corner

### Slide Type 7: Comparison Slide (Strengths)

- **Layout**: Two-column comparison (e.g., "Traditional college" vs. "Calbright")
- **Left column**: Crossed-out items in Sunset Orange (#D34508) with strikethrough styling
- **Right column**: Calbright advantages in bold, positive framing
- **Header**: "Lean into our strengths" or similar empowering phrase
- **Use**: Positioning Calbright's unique value proposition

### Slide Type 8: Quote / Testimonial Slide

- **Layout**: Large blue quotation mark graphic behind the quote text
- **Quote text**: Libre Franklin Medium or SemiBold, Midnight Black or white (depending on bg)
- **Attribution**: Smaller, in Moon Gray or Sunset Orange
- **Asset**: Use `assets/calbright-quotemark-blue.png`

### Slide Type 9: Thank You / Closing Slide

- **Background**: White or Lt Cream (#FFFCF8)
- **"Thank You!"**: Large, centered, Libre Franklin SemiBold
- **Contact info**: Below, smaller
- **Logo**: Centered below text

### General Slide Patterns

- **Accent shapes**: The template uses rounded-corner rectangles and pill shapes in brand colors
- **Numbered items**: Sky Blue (#1A23B3) circles with white numbers inside, paired with Midnight Black labels
- **Goal cards**: Blue (#1A23B3) rounded rectangles with white text inside, accented by a Cream 1 (#FFFCF8) label/tag
- **Borders**: Sunset Orange (#D34508) for emphasis borders; Light Gray (#E0E0E0) for subtle dividers

## Part 4: Application Instructions

### For Presentations (PPTX)

1. Read the pptx skill first for creation best practices
2. Use a **10" x 5.63" widescreen** slide size
3. Apply the slide type patterns from Part 3 above — mix and match for a dynamic deck
4. Color palette:
   - Title slides: White or cream background with Midnight Black title, Sunset Orange subtitle
   - Content slides: White or Lt Cream (#FFFCF8) backgrounds
   - Callout sections: Sky Blue (#1A23B3) or Med Cream (#F5EEE4)
   - Accent elements: Sunset Orange (#D34508) for highlights and CTAs
   - Supporting accent: Sunrise Yellow (#FFC629) for warm emphasis
   - Dividers/bands: Beige (#B2A490) or Light Gray (#E0E0E0)
5. Typography: Libre Franklin SemiBold for headings, Libre Franklin Regular or Medium for body
6. Logo: Use `assets/calbright-logo-primary.png` on light backgrounds, `assets/calbright-logo-white.png` on dark
7. Doodles: Add 1-2 from `assets/doodles/` per slide as corner/margin accents to keep the warm feel
8. Icons: Use from `assets/icons-orange/` or `assets/icons-blue/` for feature slides
9. Photos: Apply overlays to all full-bleed photography

### For Documents (DOCX/PDF)

1. Read the docx skill first for creation best practices
2. Headers: Libre Franklin SemiBold in Midnight Black (#1D1B1D)
3. Body: Inter Regular or Libre Franklin Regular in Moon Gray (#504F55)
4. Accents: Sunset Orange (#D34508) for callout borders and highlights, Sunrise Yellow (#FFC629) for warm emphasis
5. Backgrounds: Lt Cream (#FFFCF8) or Med Cream (#F5EEE4) for callout sections
6. Logo: Include `assets/calbright-logo-primary.png` in header or title page
7. Doodles: Use sparingly as decorative elements in margins or near section breaks
8. Use warm, accessible, student-centered language throughout

### For Spreadsheets (XLSX)

1. Read the xlsx skill first for creation best practices
2. Header rows: Sky Blue (#1A23B3) background with white text
3. Body text: Midnight Black (#1D1B1D)
4. Highlighting: Sunrise Yellow (#FFC629) for emphasis, Sunset Orange (#D34508) for alerts
5. Alternating rows: Lt Cream (#FFFCF8) and White (#FFFFFF)
6. Charts: Use Sky Blue, Sunset Orange, Sunrise Yellow as the primary series colors, with Beige (#B2A490) and Light Gray (#E0E0E0) for additional series


3. Headers: Midnight Black (#1D1B1D) in Libre Franklin SemiBold
4. Body text: Moon Gray (#504F55) in Inter Regular
5. Buttons/CTAs: Sunset Orange (#D34508) background with white text; hover: darken 10%
6. Hero sections: Sky Blue (#1A23B3) background with white text
7. Page backgrounds: White or Lt Cream (#FFFCF8)
8. Cards/sections: Med Cream (#F5EEE4) backgrounds with standard text colors
9. Links: Teal (#0097A7)
10. Rounded corners: Use `border-radius: 8px-12px` on cards and containers (matching the template's rounded rectangles)

### For Written Communications (Emails, Social, Press Materials)

1. Use the brand voice: warm, welcoming, optimistic, clear, and inclusive
2. Lead with student benefit — what Calbright makes possible for them
3. Use "Calbright College" on first reference, "Calbright" subsequently
4. Emphasize: free tuition, online flexibility, career-focused programs, support services
5. Avoid: academic jargon, bureaucratic language, anything that feels cold or institutional
6. Social media: Tag @gocalbright (Instagram, Threads, Facebook) or @calbright-college (LinkedIn)

### For All Formats

**Do:**
- Lead with the "Welcoming Spirit" — make everything feel inviting and accessible
- Use the primary color trio (Sky Blue, Sunset Orange, Sunrise Yellow) as the visual foundation
- Add hand-drawn doodles and icons to keep the feel warm and human, not corporate
- Maintain high contrast for readability and accessibility
- Use Libre Franklin for headings, Inter for body — both free from Google Fonts
- Apply image overlays to photography for brand cohesion
- Choose the correct logo version for your background color
- Reflect diversity in imagery and inclusive language in copy
- Use rounded-corner shapes, pill-shaped containers, and soft geometric elements

**Don't:**
- Make materials feel stiff, institutional, or bureaucratic
- Layer dark colors on dark or light colors on light
- Use more than 3-4 colors per layout
- Place the logo on busy/patterned backgrounds without an overlay
- Distort, recolor, or add effects to the logo or state star symbol
- Use cold, institutional, or jargon-heavy language
- Crowd the logo — always respect the "C-height" clear space rule
- Overuse doodles (1-2 per slide/page is the sweet spot)
